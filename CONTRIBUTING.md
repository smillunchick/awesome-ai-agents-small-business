# Contributing to Awesome AI Agents for Small Business

Thanks for your interest in contributing\!

## Guidelines

1. **Only add tools you have actually used or thoroughly evaluated.** No drive-by additions.
2. **One tool per PR.** Makes review easier.
3. **Include a one-line description** that explains what the tool does and why it is useful.
4. **Maintain alphabetical order** within each section.
5. **Check for duplicates** before submitting.
6. **Use the format:** `[Tool Name](https://link) â€” One-line description.`

## What to Add

- AI agent tools, frameworks, or platforms
- Resources specifically relevant to small business use cases
- Learning resources (courses, guides, tutorials)
- Communities where people discuss AI agents for business

## What Not to Add

- Generic AI tools without agent capabilities
- Tools that are no longer maintained
- Paid tools without a free trial or clear pricing
- Your own product without disclosure

## Process

1. Fork the repo
2. Add your entry in the appropriate section
3. Open a PR with a clear description
4. Wait for review

Thank you\!
